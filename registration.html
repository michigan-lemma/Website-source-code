Registration

The early registration deadline for Lemma 2019 is 11:59 PM EST on September 19, 2019. Competitors may still register after this date until 11:59 PM EST on October 11, 2019. However, only early registrants will be guaranteed a Lemma competition t-shirt.

Forms and Requirements

The contest rounds of Lemma 2019, the "propositions", are scored based on team performances. A team consists of four students, together with an adult chaperone who must be present the day of the competition. If you have a complete team, please fill out the forms below.

In order to register for Lemma 2019, please complete the following:

  1. Lemma 2019 Registration Form
  2. Release Form (Please complete one per student)
  3. Please either mail a registration fee of $X per student to SOME ADDRESS HERE.
    Note that your team will not be registered until we receive your registration fee.

Please reach out to us with any questions or concerns regarding registration!
