Registration

The registration deadline for Lemma 2019 is 11:59 PM EST on September BLAH, 2019.

Students may register for Lemma 2019 by team. A team consists of up to four students, together with an adult (18+) chaperone who must be present the day of the competition. If you do not have a complete team, you can still register! If you do not have a complete team and wish us to help match you with another incomplete team to form one team, please indicate so on section 3 of the registration form.

In order to register for Lemma 2019, please complete the following:

  1. Lemma 2019 Registration Form
  2. Release Form (Please complete one per student)
  3. Please mail a registration fee of $25 per student ($20 for early bird registration) to SOME ADDRESS HERE. Note that your team will not be registered until we receive your registration fee.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding registration, please feel free to email us!
