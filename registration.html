Registration

The early registration deadline for Lemma 2020 is 11:59 PM EST on December 13, 2019. Competitors may still register after this date until 11:59 PM EST on January 17, 2020. However, only early registrants will be guaranteed a Lemma competition t-shirt.

Forms and Requirements

Lemma Math Competition is a team-based competition. A team consists of four students, together with an adult chaperone who must be present the day of the competition. If you have a complete team, please fill out the forms below.

If you do not have enough students for a full team, do not worry! Please email us and we will attempt to accomodate you.

In order to register for Lemma 2019, please complete the following:

  1. Lemma 2019 Registration Form
  2. Release Form (Please complete one per student)
  3. Please mail a registration fee of $20 per student to 2074 East Hall, 530 Church Street, Ann Arbor, MI 48109 addressed to Michigan Lemma. If you intend to mail a check, please make it payable to Michigan Lemma. Please clearly indicate your team name and include "Lemma Registration Fee". Note that your team will not be registered until we receive your registration fee.

Please reach out to us with any questions or concerns regarding registration!
