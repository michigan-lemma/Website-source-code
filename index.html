Michigan Lemma is an Ann Arbor based mathematics competition for high school students. The first Lemma competition will take place on Saturday, January 25, 2020 at the University of Michigan Department of Mathematics.

We would like to thank our gracious sponsors: the Department of Mathematics, Jane Street Capital, and Art of Problem Solving.

About Lemma

Michigan Lemma will be a full day event during which competitors will solve challenging problems, collaborate with peers, and learn new mathematics. The contest is organized by students in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Michigan hoping to promote critical thinking and creative problem solving.

On January 25, 2020, we will hold the first annual Michigan Lemma. We aim to grow Lemma into a regional mathematics competition for midwestern high school students.
