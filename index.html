The Michigan Lemma is an Ann Arbor based mathematics competition for high school students. The first Lemma competition will take place on Saturday, October 19, 2019 at the University of Michigan Department of Mathematics.

We would like to thank our gracious sponsors: the Department of Mathematics, Jane Street Capital, and Art of Problem Solving.

About Lemma

Lemma is a local math competition hosted by the University of Michigan, aimed to open opportunities for high school students in Michigan to compete and collaborate and to disseminate a culture of problem solving and critical thinking in mathematics. The contest is organized by students in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Michigan.

In October 2019, we will hold the first annual Lemma Math Competition at the University of Michigan, with hopes that our contest will be able to grow into a regional mathematics competition for high school students in the near future.
